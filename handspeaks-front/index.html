About The Project

Our project combines cutting-edge technology with machine learning to create a smart watch capable of recognizing hand gestures. It can understand commands, making interaction with devices smoother and more intuitive.

How It Works

The gesture recognition system processes the movements of your hand and translates them into actions. This is achieved through a combination of sensors in the smartwatch and an AI-powered model that continuously improves with use.

Future Goals

In the next stages of development, we plan to enhance the gesture recognition model, integrate it with more devices, and improve its accuracy in different lighting and environmental conditions.
